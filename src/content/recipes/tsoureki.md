refer to the link in the references section.***

## Ingredients

**Yeast Blooming**
- 100 grams of yeast
- 40 grams of sugar
- 80 grams of water

**Dough**
- 300 g of unsalted butter
- 300 g of sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 2 large egg yolks
- 350 g of fresh whole milk
- 1100 g of bread flour
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 3 tears of mastixa (1 tsp of ground usually)
- 2 tsp maxlepi
- Sliced almonds (optional)

**Egg Wash Mixture**
- 1 large egg yolk
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp water
- 
## Directions

**Yeast Blooming**
1. In a small bowl combine the water, yeast and sugar.
2. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until the yeast is bloomed.

**Dough**

1. In a small saucepan in low heat, whisk together 250g of butter and the sugar until melted together and dissolved. Remove from heat.
2. In another bowl, combine the milk and the eggs and the eggs yolks and whisk until smooth. When combined, pour in the warm butter mixture. (Note that the mixure should not surpass 35$^\circ$ C to prevent melting the eggs.)
3. In the mixer bowl, sieve in the flour, the maxlepi, the mastixa, and the salt and mix til smooth.
4. Add the liquid ingredients
5. Knead the dough until it forms ropes. About 10 minutes.
6. It shouldn't stick to your hands. It should be soft, buttery, and fall apart. It should not be tight. ![[Pasted image 20241222125936.png]]
7. Melt the remaining butter. Dip your hands in and do a stretch and fold within the mixer bowl until the butter is mixed in.
8. Put it in a large bowl (it will roughly triple) and let it rise. Cover with a damp towel and let it rise for ~ 3 hours. When we dip our finger into the bowl and the indentation does not bounce back, it is ready.
9. Take the dough out from the bowl and punch out the air. Divide the pieces and fold them together as desired. Good options are braids, small buns, etc.
10. Let the dough proof after shaping for another 2 hours. They should triple in size once again.
11. Preheat the oven to 320$^\circ$ F.
12. Baste the tops of the dough in the egg wash mixture.
13. Place the buns in the oven for 20-40 minutes depending on their size.

## Notes

1. Anstrum bowl swap note the wets into the mixer bowl and then add the dries to it.
2. Anstrum reduction in flour by ~10%.
3. Don't add extra flour. Don't flour your hands.
