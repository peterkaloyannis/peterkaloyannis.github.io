## Ingredients

**Section 1**
- 1 kg of horta (chard, spinnach, etc)
- 1/3 cups of olive oil
- 4 table spoons of lemon juice
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bring a pot with some water to a boil and wash the horta well.
2. Boil the horta until softened but be careful not to overboil. Time will vary based on the crop but when I did it I did Chard and it took just a few minutes.
3. Strain and toss well with oil, lemon juice and salt to taste. 
4. Serve hot or cold.
