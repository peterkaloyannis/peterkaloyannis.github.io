I developed this recipe because I missed the taste of hot dogs from back home. I will provide a few ingredient options so that it can apply to a few different groups of people.
## Ingredients

- 6 hot dogs, preferably from [Lester's deli](https://lestersdeli.com/product/gourmet-hot-dogs/). Otherwise, a beef hot dog pack will do. Otherwise, any dog will do.
- 6 hot dog buns.
- A dill pickle relish, sweet relish can work in a pinch.
- A vinegary coleslaw, preferably from Moishes. Sauerkraut also works in a pinch.
- Yellow mustard. French's is traditional.
- 1 yellow or white onion.
- (Optional) Deli wax paper.

## Directions

1. Chop the onions into 5mm pieces. Set aside for serving later.
2. If serving for a large group: 
	1. Pour the mustard and relish into bowls and place a knives in them for easy serving.
	2. Place the coleslaw into a bowl and place a set of tongs in for easy serving.
	3. Place the onion into a bowl and use a spoon for easy serving.
3. Set up a steamer basket and place the hot dogs in. The hot dogs can steam perpetually and so they can be left in the basket.
4. When ready to serve, steam the buns for 45 seconds. Remove them from the steamer and apply a layer of mustard with the knife, followed by a hot dog, then relish, then colesaw, then onions.
5. Optionally wrap the hot dog in wax paper, skewer with a toothpick and serve immediately.
